Learning Materials

Teacher-ready and learner-ready companion materials built around speaking practice, HSK 1-3 foundations, and business starter use cases.

Teacher Guide

Run each lesson as a 30-45 minute class: character warm-up, pronunciation model, sentence substitution, pair role-play, and one recorded speaking task.

Learner Guide

Study in short daily sessions: read aloud, cover English, speak from memory, then substitute your own name, country, job, time, or company.

Assessment

Use the placement checklist to decide whether a learner should start at Zero Beginner, HSK1, HSK2, HSK3, or Business Starter.

4-Week Learning Plan

WeekThemeGoalsDaily RoutineDeliverable
Week 1Survival SpeakingGreet people, introduce yourself, say numbers, and ask simple questions.10 minutes character reading, 10 minutes phrase shadowing, 5 minutes self-introduction.Record a 30-second self-introduction.
Week 2Daily Life and TravelOrder food, ask for directions, use time words, and handle short travel exchanges.Review 20 characters, read 10 phrases aloud, complete one role-play.Complete a restaurant or taxi role-play without English notes.
Week 3HSK 1-2 Speaking BridgeTurn core words into short answers about family, work, schedule, and preferences.Read a Top 300 character set, build 5 sentences, answer 3 oral prompts.Answer 10 HSK-style personal questions aloud.
Week 4Business StarterIntroduce your company, confirm meetings, discuss price and follow-up.Practice one business script, substitute your real company details, record and revise.Deliver a 60-second business introduction and meeting follow-up.

Speaking Drill Cards

PromptChinesePinyinEnglishVariation
Introduce yourself你好，我叫 __。我是 __。nǐ hǎo, wǒ jiào __. wǒ shì __.Hello, my name is __. I am __.Replace name, country, job.
Ask someone's name你叫什么名字？nǐ jiào shén me míng zì?What is your name?Ask, listen, and answer.
Say your country我是 __ 人。wǒ shì __ rén.I am from __.Use 美国/英国/西班牙/中国.
Say what you speak我会说一点中文。wǒ huì shuō yì diǎn zhōng wén.I can speak a little Chinese.Change 中文 to 英文.
Ask for time现在几点？xiàn zài jǐ diǎn?What time is it now?Answer with __ 点.
Order tea我要一杯茶。wǒ yào yī bēi chá.I would like a cup of tea.Change tea to water or coffee.
Ask for directions请问，地铁站怎么走？qǐng wèn, dì tiě zhàn zěn me zǒu?Excuse me, how do I get to the subway station?Change destination.
Confirm a meeting请确认会议时间。qǐng què rèn huì yì shí jiān.Please confirm the meeting time.Add tomorrow or afternoon.
Reply later我稍后回复你。wǒ shāo hòu huí fù nǐ.I will reply to you later.Use in a business chat.
Ask for price这个多少钱？zhè ge duō shǎo qián?How much is this?Change this to product/service.
Ask for discount可以有折扣吗？kě yǐ yǒu zhé kòu ma?Can there be a discount?Practice polite tone.
Give an opinion我觉得这个很好。wǒ jué de zhè ge hěn hǎo.I think this is very good.Change adjective.
Give a reason因为今天很忙，所以我不能去。yīn wèi jīn tiān hěn máng, suǒ yǐ wǒ bù néng qù.Because I am busy today, I cannot go.Change reason.
Change a plan我们需要改变计划。wǒ men xū yào gǎi biàn jì huà.We need to change the plan.Add time or reason.
Introduce role我负责中国市场。wǒ fù zé zhōng guó shì chǎng.I am responsible for the China market.Change market/department.
